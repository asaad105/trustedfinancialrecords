About Us

Trusted Financial Records Company helps small to mid-sized tech startups in Calgary, Alberta build reliable financial systems so they can focus on growth. We combine bookkeeping precision with practical startup experience to keep your records clean, compliant, and decision-ready.

Meet the Founder

Trusted Financial Records Company was founded by a bookkeeping professional who saw fast-growing startups struggling to turn raw numbers into clear financial direction. After working with founders through early fundraising, team expansion, and tax season pressure, our founder built this company around one promise: every entrepreneur deserves timely, transparent records they can trust.

Our Method of Working

Our method starts with discovery, then moves into setup, monthly execution, and performance reviews. We use secure cloud accounting tools, structured reconciliations, and plain-language reporting so founders always know cash position, runway, and obligations. Every client gets a repeatable process, proactive reminders, and direct access to guidance when decisions need to be made quickly.

What Makes Us Different

Partnership Mentality
