Frequently Asked Questions

Can we keep control of invoice approvals and bank access?

Yes. Approval authority and payment release rights remain with your designated internal decision-makers. We operate inside that structure.

Is this suitable for smaller businesses?

Yes. The service is designed for companies that need strong AP discipline and accurate books but do not need a large internal finance department.

How do you handle urgent or delayed vendor payments?

We escalate urgent items fast, provide payment-priority context, and coordinate communication so vendors receive clear timelines.
