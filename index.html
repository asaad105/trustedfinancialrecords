Frequently Asked Questions

Can we keep control of invoice approvals?

Yes. Your team controls who approves, spending limits, and final authorization. We operate the process within those rules.

Do you work with smaller companies?

Yes. Our focus is small and growing organizations that need dependable finance operations without a full in-house department.

What happens if a payment must be delayed?

We flag due dates early, align with your cash plan, and help communicate updated timelines to vendors when needed.
